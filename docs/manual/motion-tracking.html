Time & Movement Tracking

Implemented Measured Final_Eye seals UTC time on every motion tick and estimates movement from silent frame fingerprints — motion_score, velocity, direction, stationary streak. Receipts append to data/eye-motion.jsonl. High motion can auto-witness into eye stoard.

Quick start

pythong gui/app.py
curl -s http://127.0.0.1:9479/api/eye/motion | jq .
curl -s -X POST http://127.0.0.1:9479/api/eye/motion/tick | jq .
curl -s -X POST http://127.0.0.1:9479/api/eye/motion/start \
  -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"interval_sec":2}' | jq .

API reference

  • GET /api/eye/motion — session status, last kinematics
  • GET /api/eye/motion/doctrine — thresholds and policy
  • GET /api/eye/motion/ledger?n=30 — recent ticks
  • POST /api/eye/motion/tick — one sealed sample
  • POST /api/eye/motion/start — background loop
  • POST /api/eye/motion/stop — halt loop

Time discipline

Every tick carries sealed_ts (UTC ISO). Session elapsed_sec uses monotonic clock. When NEXUS_STATE_DIR is set, sovereign pulse metadata is included from sovereign-time-anchor.json — correlate with NEXUS panel receipts.

Movement kinematics

Frames come from the silent preserve cascade (never flashy tools). Perceptual hash delta yields motion_score (0–1). Centroid drift yields velocity and direction_deg. Alerts fire above doctrine threshold (default 0.12); stoard witness on alert when enabled.

Grep the ledger

grep motion_tick data/eye-motion.jsonl | tail -5
jq '.kinematics.level' data/eye-motion.jsonl | tail -20
